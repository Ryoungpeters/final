Bern, Switzerland

Bern is a peaceful, charming capital city known for its medieval architecture, riverside views, and historic old town. It’s calm, beautiful, and full of character.

Destination Information

Bern’s Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is full of cobblestone streets, clock towers, fountains, and historic buildings. The city has a relaxed atmosphere that makes it easy to explore at your own pace.

Travel Tips & Logistics

Switzerland is known for its efficiency, and Bern’s public transport is no different. Trams and buses make it simple to get around. Because Switzerland can be pricey, budgeting ahead helps. Bern is very walkable, and the river paths offer great views.

Fun Things to Do

Visit the famous Zytglogge clock tower, relax along the Aare River, explore the Bear Park, or tour the city’s museums. Bern also has amazing chocolate shops and cozy cafés worth trying.
