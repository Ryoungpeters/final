Paris, France

Discover the beauty, culture, and iconic landmarks of Paris — the world’s most romantic and artistic city.

Destination Information

Paris is a city full of history, character, and beautiful scenery. Every part of the city feels different, from quiet side streets and cafés to busy areas filled with museums, shops, and landmarks. No matter where you go, there’s always something interesting to see or experience, which is why people from all over the world visit every year.

Travel Tips & Logistics

Getting around Paris is actually really simple. The metro is fast, cheap, and takes you almost anywhere you want to go. A lot of major attractions offer timed tickets, so booking ahead can save you a long wait in line. Paris is very walkable, so comfortable shoes help a lot. Even knowing a few basic French phrases can make your trip smoother and more enjoyable.

Fun Things to Do

Paris has a ton of fun things to do no matter what you're into. You can relax with a picnic near the Eiffel Tower, take a boat ride along the Seine, or explore the streets around Notre-Dame. The food scene is amazing — from bakeries to local markets — and the parks and museums are always worth checking out. No matter what you choose, Paris has something memorable to offer.
